#1 NBA Star who live extravagant lifestyle

Feb 20, 2022

There is literally zero surprise here as LeBron has dominated this era of basketball and will go down as one of the two greatest players to every grace a basketball court. James has been in the league for 18 years and has racked up 4 MVP awards and 4 NBA titles. LeBron is a very special talent on the court, and he loves to show off his luxury lifestyle off of it. James has had 3 major houses during his time in the league and we will start with his first naturally, the 18 year old James purchased a very large plot of land in Akron that would soon transform into a 30,000 square foot mansion that has 19 rooms, 6 bedrooms, a recording studio, a bowling alley, an on line casino, an aquarium, a barbershop and a 2-story walk in closet. That last line may be the most luxurious line I have ever read, but wait, there is more. James also purchased a house in his time with the Miami Heat for $13.4 million and most recently bought a $21 million house in California. On top of all this, LeBron has a car collection that would make your jaw drop, and is going to keep racking up money from his endorsements for the rest of his life. LeBron James and his outrageous sums of money have landed him the most luxurious lifestyle in the NBA.
